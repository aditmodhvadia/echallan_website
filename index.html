About E-Challan

e-challan is an electronic format of the challan. An e-challan can also be defined as a specific format used for depositing or remitting the contribution or statutory payment at a bank or treasury. A challan is a way of crediting the money to one's bank account through a form.

The E-Challan Initiative

An example of a challan would be spot traffic ticket issued by the traffic police for a violation. This challan would then have to be paid directly by cash, at an e-seva center, or by any other payment mode as specified on the challan. Government of Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh has also introduced mechanism to enable payment of e-challans with the help of PDAs on road by commuters for their pending e-challans.
